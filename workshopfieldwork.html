•A Field Workshop: A Regional Geological Evaluation of the Western and Central Pontides: Insight into the opening of the Western Black Sea and Subsurface Models for the Han Asparuh Block, Bulgaria. Organized by Neftex Petroleum Consultants Company, UK.

•A Field Workshop: Evolution of the Central Anatolian Plateau and its Northern and Southern Margins. Organized by Universität Potsdam and İstanbul Technical University. Field Trip: Kastamonu, Sinop, Salt Lake/Turkey.
      20-29 September 2013.
         →10 days of field excursion which is focused on tectonic morphology of Central Anatolian Plateau and bounding regions.
         →Gained experience during crossing Turkey from north to south, observing Pontides, North Anatolian Fault Zone, Tethys Sutures, volcanic units of Central Anatolia, fluvial morphology of Kızılırmak and Miocene evolution of middle Taurus Mountains.
